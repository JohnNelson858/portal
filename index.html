About Me

I am an electronics student and aspiring application developer with experience in programming, troubleshooting, and technical problem solving. I enjoy building practical solutions that combine software, electronics, and real-world applications.

After originally earning a Computer Programming degree in the 1980s, I returned to school to update my skills and prepare for today's technology industry. I am currently completing an Associate of Applied Science degree in Electronics while studying web development and application programming.

My coursework includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GitHub collaboration, application development, programmable logic controllers, electronics troubleshooting, and technical documentation. These experiences have strengthened my ability to analyze problems and develop effective solutions.

To see examples of my work, visit my Projects page or explore my GitHub repositories. There you can view web development assignments, programming projects, and other technical work that demonstrates my growing skills and experience.

When I am not studying or building projects, I enjoy fishing, camping, barbeques, movies, and learning about entrepreneurship. My long-term goal is to combine my technical background and business interests to create practical and sustainable ventures of my own.
